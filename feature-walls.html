Feature Walls

Make Your Space Stand Out.

A well-executed feature wall is one of the most effective ways to transform a room without a full renovation. Whether you're after a deep, rich colour behind a bed head, a textured finish in a living area, or a bold brand statement in a commercial space — we can make it happen.

We'll help you choose the right finish for the space, properly prepare the surface, and apply it with the care and precision a statement wall deserves.

What we offer:

  • Solid colour feature walls — any shade, any finish
  • Two-tone and colour blocking techniques
  • Limewash and venetian plaster finishes
  • Textured and suede paint effects
  • Geometric and panelled wall designs
  • Commercial feature walls and brand colour applications
  • Colour consultation if you're not sure where to start

Not Sure on the Colour?

Choosing a feature wall colour can be daunting. We've painted hundreds of them and are happy to advise — what works with your existing colours, what reads well in natural light, and what finishes hold up over time.

We can also do small test patches so you can see the colour on your actual wall before we commit to the full job.
