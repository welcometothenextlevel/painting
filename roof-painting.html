Roof Painting

Protect Your Roof. Save Thousands.

In Perth, roofs take a beating. Years of sun, heat, and the occasional storm cause colour to fade, surface coatings to crack, and tiles to become porous. Roof painting is a cost-effective way to restore your roof's appearance and — more importantly — seal and protect it before the damage gets worse.

Done right, a roof paint job can extend the life of your roof by a decade or more. Done wrong, it peels within a year. We use specialist roof coatings and thorough preparation — pressure washing, priming, and sealing — before any colour goes on.

Roof types we work on:

  • Terracotta roof tiles
  • Concrete roof tiles
  • Corrugated metal (Colourbond and older iron)
  • Flat and low-pitch roofs
  • Fibrous cement (Fibro)
  • Facia boards and gutters (while up there)

What Changes. What Doesn't.

Roof painting restores colour and seals surface porosity — it won't fix structural damage or replace broken tiles. We'll be upfront about what we find during our assessment so you can make an informed decision.

A quality roof repaint includes a thorough high-pressure wash, a sealer/primer coat, and two coats of a specialist roof membrane or tile paint — not standard exterior paint.

Our Process

01

Free Roof Assessment

We inspect the roof condition, identify any repairs needed, and quote you accurately for the full job.

02

High-Pressure Wash

All moss, lichen, dirt, and loose coating removed. The roof must be clean for paint to bond properly.

03

Seal & Prime

A sealer/primer coat penetrates porous tiles and creates a proper base for the colour coats.

04

Two Colour Coats

Specialist roof membrane applied in your chosen colour. Even coverage, all edges and ridges included.
